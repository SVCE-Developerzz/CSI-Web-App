ANNUAL REPORT(2017-18)


CSI-SVCE Student chapter consists of 3 faculty nominees and 261 student volunteers.

Number of student volunteers included in the academic year 2017-18 including 147 IT students and 1 EEE student.


GUEST LECTURES

CSI- SVCE student chapter had organized a guest lecture on 7th October 2017, Mr. Tony Caleb, Executive Director from Infoziant Systems Chennai, delivered a guest Lecture on “Penetration Testing and Malware Analysis” during the inaugural function of CSI-SVCE student chapter.


CSI-SVCE student chapter in association with Department of Information Technology had also organized a guest lecture on 28th December 2017, Ms. Lavanya S. P, Deputy Superintendent of Police from Cyber Cell Crime branch delivered a guest lecture on "Cyber Forensics".
